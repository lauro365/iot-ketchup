# Homemade Ketchup Recipe

A classic condiment perfect for demonstrating collaborative editing!

## Ingredients

- 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 cup white vinegar
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan
2. Whisk until smooth
3. Bring to a boil over medium heat
4. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally
5. Let cool and transfer to a bottle or jar
6. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving

## Tips

- Adjust sugar and vinegar to taste
- Keeps in refrigerator for up to 1 month
- Try adding cayenne pepper for a spicy version
